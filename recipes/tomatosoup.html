Tomato Soup

This soup goes great with the tomato soup. Be sure to add some basil for extra flavor


See below for how to make this delicious soup!

Ingredients

  • crushed tomatoes
  • butter
  • onion
  • water

Steps

  1. Heat pan on stove to medium high heat, melt butter
  2. Add onion, sweat for a few minutes
  3. add tomatoes and water
  4. Cook until done
