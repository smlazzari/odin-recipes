Grilled Cheese

This grilled cheese is delicious. I mean, it is cheese and bread; what could be better??


This recipe is really simple, too. All you need is bread, cheese, and butter.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Heat up frying pan to medium heat, and add butter
  2. Butter one slice of bread and place in pan, butter side down
  3. place cheese on top of bread in pan
  4. butter other piece of bread, and place on top of cheese, butter side up
  5. Check bottom of bread in pan. If it is browned to your liking then flip
  6. Once other side of bread is cooked to your liking, remove from pan
  7. plate your sandwich
  8. Cut sandwich into two triangles - because only socio-paths cut sandwiches into squares
  9. enjoy!
