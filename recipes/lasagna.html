Lasagna

This lasagna was made by a lady, Brenda. You can find the recipe here Brenda worked really hard on the recipe, so I wanted to give her credit.


You can make your own lasagna too by following the recipe below. First look at the steps, then look at the instructions


Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Lots of Mozzerella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta, and cook for 10 minutes
  3. Take baking dish and put a layer of sauce in the bottom
  4. Put a layer of noodles and then a layer of cheese
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4
  6. put in 425 degree oven for 45 mins
